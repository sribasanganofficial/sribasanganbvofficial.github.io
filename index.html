What Makes Us Unique

We Educate

We offer spiritual and cultural education to all age groups with workshops, classes, and community outreach.

We Serve

Through various charitable events and support programs, we uplift the underprivileged and serve society selflessly.

We Celebrate

Our community rejoices in festivals, kirtans, and spiritual gatherings to keep the devotional flame alive.

Spiritual & Cultural Activities

Weekly Bhakti Vriksha

Regular spiritual gatherings with kirtans, Bhagavad Gita discussions, and prasadam sharing in devotees' homes.

Festival Celebrations

Joyful observances of Gaura Purnima, Janmashtami, Ratha Yatra and more with devotion and grandeur.

Sunday Love Feast

Weekly spiritual festivals with bhajans, lectures, drama, and free prasadam for all visitors.

Spiritual Library

Access a wide range of Vedic literature including Bhagavad Gita, Srimad Bhagavatam, and books by Srila Prabhupada.

Online Discourses

Join live spiritual talks, video classes, and Q&A sessions through our YouTube and Telegram platforms.

Youth & Kids Programs

Dedicated sessions for children and teens focusing on values, storytelling, and cultural arts.
