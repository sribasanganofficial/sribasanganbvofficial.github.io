🕊️ Sri Krishna Janmashtami

Every year, we joyfully commemorate the birth of Sri Krishna with a spiritually immersive experience. The festival begins at midnight—the moment of His divine appearance—when our temple resounds with soul-stirring kirtan, devotional bhajans, and Bhagavad-Gita readings. Elaborate Rasa-lila dramas are performed, depicting Krishna’s or divine pastimes, while the deities are dressed in exquisite attire. A vibrant prasadam feast follows, which nourishes both body and heart. As a hub of devotion in Kolkata, Sribas Angan brings together families in chanting, dancing, and prayer, fostering deep spiritual connection. Your heartfelt contribution today helps us continue this beautiful tradition and supports the free prasadam that feeds thousands—please consider donating to sustain our Janmashtami celebrations.

🎉 Jhulan Utsava (Swing Festival)

In the month of Shravan, our deities Sri Radha–Krishna are exquisitely adorned with flowers and seated on a grand swing that is gently rocked to the tunes of wistful kirtans. Each swing session is accompanied by devotional singing, colorful decoration, and a serene atmosphere that evokes the loving exchanges between Radha and Krishna. Families come together to offer their own small swings and garlands. Your generous support allows us to maintain the charming swing decorations and prasadam offerings that make this festival a truly magical home-centric experience. Donations help keep the devotional ambience alive—every gift counts toward enriching our community gatherings.

🛕 Sri Gaura Purnima

Celebrating the divine advent of Sri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu, this joyous festival radiates devotional unity and chanting. Over the course of the day, we conduct all-day sankirtan, offered with deep reverence, interspersed with uplifting scriptural discussions on the Mahaprabhu’s teachings. At moonrise, ornate prasadam prasadam is distributed as participants chant in ecstasy. At Sribas Angan, Gaura Purnima unites hearts in ecstatic love and devotional service. Your contribution ensures we can arrange heartfelt kirtans and provide nourishing prasadam to all attendees—please donate today to support this sacred celebration.

🛤️ Ratha-Yatra (Festival of Chariots)

Once a year, during the festive month of Ashadha, we proudly process the Lord Jagannath deities, Lord Baladeva, and Srimati Subhadra on chariots through the streets of Kolkata. Our temple premises transform into a vibrant atmosphere with kirtan, festoons, and prasadam distribution, accompanied by cultural performances celebrating Krishna’s pastimes. This annual tradition bridges the temple with the community, inviting all to experience devotional joy. Contributing to this event allows us to decorate chariots, distribute prasadam, and manage large crowds safely—please consider donating to support Kolkata’s Grand Ratha-Yatra.

🌄 Govardhan Puja & Annakut

On the first lunar day after Diwali, we celebrate Sri Krishna’s lifting of Govardhan Hill. Our temple grounds are transformed into a mountain of food—Annakut—comprised of dozens of handcrafted vegetarian dishes offered to the Lord. Each food item symbolizes gratitude for Krishna’s protection and sustenance. Music and discourse deepen the spiritual resonance of the occasion. Your donation helps us prepare elaborate Annakut arrangements and prasadam that nourish hundreds—it’s a beautiful chance to support devotional service and food sanctification.

📚 Gita Jayanti

Designated as the appearance day of the Bhagavad-Gita, this celebration features immersive yajna offerings, Gita recitations, and animated discussion sessions highlighting Krishna’s teachings. Participants receive copies of the Gita along with compassionate prasadam, fostering spiritual engagement and community learning. Sribas Angan strives to bring this wisdom to all. Your gift provides reading materials, sattvic prasadam, and educational support—for the love of Krishna, please donate to help expand our program’s reach.

🕯️ Deepdaan Utsav (Kartika Month Lights)

During the auspicious Kartika month, our homes and temple are beautifully illuminated with ceremonial lamps and Deepdan offerings in the tradition of Damodara-lila. Volunteers visit local homes nightly, offering kirtans and sowing devotional warmth across Kolkata’s neighborhoods. Each lamp represents devotion, each song a reminder of Krishna’s grace. Your generosity enables us to purchase lamps, distribute prasadam, and expand this devotional outreach—please donate to brighten homes and hearts this Kartika.

🪔 Light a Deep & Brighten a Home

🎊 Sri Rama Navami

Honoring the divine birth of Lord Rama, we hold serene Bhagavata Parayan, heartfelt discourses, and kirtans extolling Lord Rama’s virtues of righteousness, courage, and humility. Our temple is lovingly decorated, and sanctified meals are distributed in celebration. Rama's example inspires our communities toward dharma and devotion. Your gift helps us sustain this festival and support the sharing of nutritious prasadam—please contribute in support of Rama Navami’s uplifting spirit.

🎂 Vyasa Puja (Srila Prabhupada's Appearance Day)

On Vyasa Puja, we honor His Divine Grace Srila Prabhupada, whose life brought Krishna Consciousness to Kolkata and the world. We hold devotional arati, share heartfelt testimonials, and host cultural programs reflecting his missionary spirit. A free prasadam feast is served as an offering of gratitude. Your support helps us organize this remembrance and encourage future generations of devotees—please donate to honor Srila Prabhupada’s legacy.

🌺 Radhashtami

Celebrated as the appearance day of Srimati Radharani, our festival is imbued with love and devotion. We adorn the deity with floral decorations, recite Radha-stava liturgies, sing devotees’ hymns, and serve prasadam in her name. Each offering symbolizes the soul’s longing for God’s mercy. You can help us sustain this joyful festival—please donate to support Radhashtami’s floral alankars and sumptuous prasadam.

🤍 Sri Nityananda Trayodashi

We celebrate Lord Nityananda Prabhu—the merciful elder brother of Lord Chaitanya—who reveals all mercy through His holy name. Festivities include vibrant kirtan, abhishek, and soul-stirring discourses on His compassionate mission. Annaprasadam (food distribution) continues during the festival. Your donation helps spread His mercy to all—please donate to support our Nityananda Trayodashi programs.
